## Conversion Rate Targets

### Affiliate Conversion Rates (Benchmarks)

| Stage | Target | Method |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Site visitor → Article page | 40% | Internal linking |
| Article reader → Affiliate link click | 2-5% | Relevant CTAs |
| Affiliate click → Signup | 5-10% | Quality traffic |
| **Overall conversion**: | 0.4-2.5% | Industry average 0.5-2% |

**Money calculation**:
- 5,000 monthly visitors
- 40% read comparison articles (2,000)
- 3% click affiliate link (60 clicks)
- 8% convert to signup (5 signups)
- $2-5 per signup × 5 = **$10-25/month**

At 1% overall conversion rate with $3 average commission:
- 5,000 visitors × 1% = 50 conversions × $3 = **$150/month**

## Risk Mitigation

### Revenue Concentration Risk

**Problem**: Relying too heavily on one affiliate program

**Solution**:
- Maintain 5+ affiliate programs
- No single program > 30% of revenue
- Diversify across product categories

### Algorithm Risk (Google SEO Changes)

**Problem**: Google algorithm update kills traffic

**Solution**:
- Build email list (own audience)
- Create content clusters for E-E-A-T (Expertise, Experience, Authority, Trust)
- Monitor SEO metrics monthly
- Maintain 3+ content pillars

### Affiliate Program Risk

**Problem**: Affiliate program shuts down or reduces commission

**Solution**:
- Pre-vet all affiliate programs
- Have 2-3 backup programs per category
- Monitor commission changes

### Click Fraud Risk

**Problem**: Invalid traffic clicks hurting conversion metrics

**Solution**:
- Use Google Analytics 4 fraud detection
- Monitor CTR abnormalities
- Only promote to genuine readers

## Compliance & Disclosure

### FTC/Japan Consumer Affairs Guidelines

- [ ] Clearly label affiliate links with disclaimer
- [ ] Include affiliate disclosure on site footer
- [ ] Use affiliate link formatting consistently
- [ ] Disclose sponsored content immediately
- [ ] No misleading comparisons (fair & honest)

### Recommended Footer Text

```
Affiliate Disclosure: This website participates in affiliate programs 
and earns commissions on qualifying purchases. We recommend products 
we believe provide genuine value. Your use of these links supports 
our continued operation. Prices unchanged for you.
```

### Japan-Specific Requirements

- 景品表示法 (Act against Unjustifiable Premiums and Misleading Representations): Clear affiliate disclosure
- 特定商取引に関する法律 (Act on Specified Commercial Transaction): 
  If accepting payments, include required info
- APPI (Act on the Protection of Personal Information): Privacy policy
